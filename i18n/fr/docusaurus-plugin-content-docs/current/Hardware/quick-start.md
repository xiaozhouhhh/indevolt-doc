# Quick Start



1. Expansion battery mounted 4 support feet on the bottom.
2. Place an expansion battery 20~30 mm from the wall on the floor.
3. Adjust the height of the four support feet at the bottom of the equipment to ensure it is in a level position, calibrate using a level.
4. Remove the bottom protective cap of the power module, As well as removing the protective caps at the top and bottom of the middle battery module.

Please be careful not to remove the protective cap of the bottom most expansion battery to avoid damage to the device!

5. Stack the expansion battery packs one after another with the power module on top, connecting the two corresponding connectors.
6. Attach the L-shaped wall bracket to both sides of the first expansion battery under the power module using M4 Phillips screws.
7. After the power module and battery module are installed, the 2 modules are fixed by M4 Phillips screws.
8. Mark the position of the drill holes on both sides and use a drill with a Ã˜8 drill bit and a depth of 60 mm.
9. Use a hammer to insert the plastic sleeve of the M5x60 self-tapping screw into the hole, and then use a Phillips screwdriver to fasten the M5x60 self-tapping screw to the L-shaped wall mounting bracket.

This product contains a dual isolation transformer that complies with safety requirements without requiring measurement of the array grounding insulation resistance or detection of array fault currents.
