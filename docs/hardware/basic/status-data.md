# View Status and Data

You can check the current operating status of your device and view household generation, storage, and consumption statistics anytime in the INDEVOLT App.

- **View Device Status**  
  After adding the device in the app, tap the corresponding device card on the device page to enter [Device Details](../../app/device.md#2-device-details) and view network status, operating mode, current power, and other information.

- **View Energy Generation and Consumption Data**  
  In the bottom navigation bar of the app, tap [Report](../../app/report.md) to open the household energy overview page. You can view daily / weekly / monthly / yearly energy consumption trends, solar generation, and estimated electricity cost savings.
