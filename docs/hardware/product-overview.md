# Product Overview

The Indevolt Micro Energy Storage System is a **plug-and-play residential micro energy storage solution**, typically used in combination with PV modules.

The system stores surplus solar energy generated during the day and releases it when needed to power household loads. This increases solar self-consumption and reduces reliance on the grid.

No complex construction or modification of the existing electrical distribution system is required, making it ideal for households seeking an accessible and low-barrier clean energy solution.

## Typical Application Scenarios

- ✅ Apartments or homes with limited balcony space  
- ✅ Users unable to install large rooftop PV systems  
- ✅ Regions in the EU with grid feed-in power limits (e.g., 600W / 800W / 2000W)  
- ✅ Households aiming to increase solar self-consumption and reduce grid electricity purchases  
- ✅ Environments with time-of-use or peak electricity pricing, where pricing strategies can help reduce energy costs  

## Product Advantages

- 🔌 Plug-and-play installation with no complex setup required  
- 🖥️ Supports local operation without cloud dependency  
- 🔋 Modular design with scalable storage capacity  
- 🛡️ Compliant with EU safety and grid-connection standards  
- 📱 Real-time monitoring and energy strategy adjustment via the app  
- 🔗 Provides a local API for integration with third-party platforms or smart home systems, enabling advanced automation control
