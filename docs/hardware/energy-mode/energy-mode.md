# Energy Management

Energy management is used to control the charging and discharging behavior of the energy storage system under different time periods and load conditions.

By selecting an appropriate energy usage mode, you can determine **where electricity comes from, when it is used, and how it is distributed**, thereby improving energy efficiency and reducing electricity costs.

## What Can Energy Management Do?

Through energy management, you can:

- Prioritize the use of household solar PV generation and reduce grid electricity consumption  
- Charge or discharge the battery at optimal times  
- Optimize energy costs based on usage habits or electricity pricing changes  
- Manually or automatically control system operating behavior when needed  

The system provides multiple energy modes to meet different household scenarios.
