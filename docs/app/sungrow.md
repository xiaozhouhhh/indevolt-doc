# How to Get the Sungrow Appkey and Secret key

Follow the steps below to create an application in the Sungrow Developer Portal and obtain your **Appkey** and **Secret key**.

1. Go to https://web3.isolarcloud.eu/#/login and sign in with your iSolarCloud account.

   <img src={require("./img/sungrow1.png").default} />

2. Click the <img src={require("./img/sungrow_icon.png").default} width="20" style={{verticalAlign: "middle"}}/>  in the upper-right corner, then select **Developer portal**.

   <img src={require("./img/sungrow2.png").default} />

3. After entering the [Developer Portal](https://developer-api.isolarcloud.com/#/home), click **Applications** in the upper-right corner to open the **My applications** page.

   <img src={require("./img/sungrow3.png").default} />

4. Click **Create application** to create a new application.

   <img src={require("./img/sungrow4.png").default} />

5. Fill in the application information:

   - **Application name**: Enter the app name (INDEVOLT) or another recognizable name, such as `My Home EMS`.
   - **Company name**: Enter the name of the home created in the app or another suitable identifier, such as `My Home`.
   - **Application description**: Enter a brief description, such as `Data Integration`.
   - **Authorize with OAuth2.0**: Select **No**

   Then click **Create application**.

   <img src={require("./img/sungrow5.png").default} />

6. Return to the My applications page and click **View** for the application you just created.
   <img src={require("./img/sungrow6.png").default} />

7. Your **Appkey** and **Secret key** are displayed on the application details page.
   <img src={require("./img/sungrow7.png").default} />
